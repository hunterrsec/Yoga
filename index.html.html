Our Story

Where the Ocean
Meets the Soul

Polem Beach Yoga was born from a simple belief: that the ocean has the power to heal, and yoga has the power to transform. Nestled on the secluded southern shores of Goa, our sanctuary offers an escape from the noise of modern life.

Founded by a collective of master yoga teachers, Ayurvedic practitioners, and hospitality visionaries, we have created a space where ancient wisdom meets contemporary luxury. Every detail—from the organic meals to the handwoven textiles—has been chosen to support your journey inward.

We host only 12 guests at a time, ensuring personalized attention and an intimate community atmosphere. This is not a resort. This is a pilgrimage to your truest self.

Terms & Conditions

Booking & Payment

A 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your reservation. The remaining balance is due 14 days prior to arrival. All payments are processed securely. Prices are inclusive of accommodation, meals, yoga sessions, and scheduled activities. Airfare and airport transfers are not included unless specified.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 30+ days before arrival receive a 50% refund of the deposit. Cancellations 14-29 days before receive a 25% refund. Cancellations within 14 days are non-refundable. We strongly recommend travel insurance. In the unlikely event we must cancel, a full refund will be provided.

Refund Policy

No refunds for early departure or unused services. If you extend your stay, additional nights are subject to availability and standard rates. Gift certificates are non-refundable but transferable.

Medical Disclaimer

Yoga and wellness activities involve physical exertion. Please consult your physician before participating. You must disclose any medical conditions, injuries, or pregnancies at the time of booking. We reserve the right to restrict participation in certain activities for your safety.

Liability Waiver

Participation in all activities is at your own risk. Polem Beach Yoga and its instructors are not liable for any injury, loss, or damage to personal property. We maintain comprehensive insurance and adhere to highest safety standards.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Personal information collected during booking is used solely for reservation purposes and will never be sold to third parties. We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. By using our website, you consent to our privacy practices.
